Title: The Principles of LSS published

The first public document in the new Lunar Scripts System documentation area has been published.

[The Principles of LSS](../docs-lss-principles.html) describes the engineering values that should be preserved as the system evolves: staying close to Linux, keeping software close to upstream, understanding before automation, readable engineering and the preservation of technical knowledge.

The document is available through the new [LSS documentation hub](../lss.html).
