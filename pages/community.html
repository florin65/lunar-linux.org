Community

Small community. Shared knowledge.

Built by people who use it

Lunar Linux is developed and maintained by a small community of Linux users, administrators and developers.

Unlike large distributions with thousands of users and multiple support layers, Lunar Linux keeps communication direct. Users, contributors and developers often meet in the same channels and discussions.

In Lunar Linux, community is not separate from development. The people who use the system are often the people who improve it.
